Communities

Connect with fellow students and alumni who share your interests.

A place for all things web development: front-end, back-end, and full-stack. Share projects, ask for help, and stay updated on the latest trends.

Find a team for your next hackathon, share ideas, and celebrate your wins. This community is for innovators and problem-solvers.

For students and alumni with a startup mindset. Share business ideas, find co-founders, and get advice from successful entrepreneurs.
